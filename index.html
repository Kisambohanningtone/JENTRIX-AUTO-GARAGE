🚗 About Us

Jentrix Auto Garage is a trusted automotive service center located in Ruaka, Nairobi, dedicated to delivering quality workmanship and reliable vehicle care.

We specialize in a wide range of services including professional car paint, detailing, car accessories installation, accident repair, wheel alignment, and auto brake services. Our team is committed to restoring your vehicle’s performance, safety, and appearance to the highest standards.

At Jentrix Auto Garage, we understand that your car is more than just transportation—it’s an essential part of your daily life. That’s why we focus on precision, efficiency, and customer satisfaction in every service we provide.

Whether it’s a minor touch-up or major repair, you can count on us for honest service, skilled expertise, and results that last.

Our Services

Original Spray Paint

Using advanced color-matching technology for a perfect, factory-standard finish. We restore your car's original aesthetic after a scratch or a full respray.

Light Diagnostic & Wiring

Troubleshoot and fix all electrical and lighting systems, from simple bulb replacements to complex wiring issues.

Suspension Repairs

From shock absorbers to ball joints, we diagnose and repair all suspension issues to ensure a comfortable and safe ride.

Major Mechanical Repairs

Don't let a major issue sideline you. Our team is equipped to handle complex engine, transmission, and drivetrain repairs with precision and efficiency.

Regular Engine Service
